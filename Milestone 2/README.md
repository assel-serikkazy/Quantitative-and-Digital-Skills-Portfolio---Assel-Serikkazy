Project Description:
This project analyzes a company's client database segmented by deciles. The analysis focuses on revenue, profit, and customer behavior across deciles to identify high-value clients and assess their contribution to overall business performance.

Analysis Includes: 
- Customer segmentation by decile  
- Calculation of key metrics: Average revenue per customer, Average profit per customer, Order frequency, Order value, Profit margin  
- Visualizations: Revenue and Profit by decile, Average Margin by decile  
- Insights: Top deciles contribute disproportionately to revenue and profit; high-value clients transact more frequently and with higher order value  

Skills Demonstrated:
- Data cleaning and structuring in Excel  
- Quantitative analysis and metric calculation  
- Data visualization and interpretation  
- Business insight generation suitable for professional applications
